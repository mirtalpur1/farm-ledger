Assumptions & notes
  • Shared inputs = Fertilizer + Seed + Pesticide. Each party's obligation = 50% of that total.
  • Sharecropper is 100% responsible for Ploughing & Labour (his profit share is first used to cover this).
  • Reimbursements are applied only when the sharecropper paid items. Loans during cultivation are adjusted at the end.
  • Negative net profit indicates a loss.
